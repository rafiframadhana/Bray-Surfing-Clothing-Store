Shipping Information

Shipping Methods


We offer standard shipping and express shipping options for domestic orders. The shipping method and cost will be displayed at checkout based on your location and the items in your cart.

Shipping Times


Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days for domestic orders. Express shipping is available for faster delivery.

Tracking Your Order


Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to track the status of your shipment.

Shipping Costs


Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight of your order and your location. The shipping cost will be displayed at checkout before you complete your purchase.

Contact Us


If you have any questions about our shipping policies or need assistance with your order, contact us at braysurfingstore@gmail.com.
